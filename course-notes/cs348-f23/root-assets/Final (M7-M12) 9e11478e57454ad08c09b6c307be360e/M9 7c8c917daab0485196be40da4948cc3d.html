Problem

  1. Redundancy
  1. Loses Information

Solution

Design a schema in a normal form

Normal Forms

General Idea

To ensure non-redundant representation of data that does not lose info:

  • Ensures that facts about different entities are in separate tables
    • Each schema should has 2 sets of attributes:
      • PK
      • independent facts about the entity

BCNF vs. 3NF

  • They both always create a lossless-join decomposition
  • You can always find one that preserves dependencies with 3NF, not always possible with BCNF

Minimal Cover

Simple sets are desirable
