Value (aka. Utility)

During the process of gathering rewards, the agent needs to quantify the value of its decisions (aka utility). Some options are:

  • Total / Average Reward ❌

    Problem: doesn’t work for indefinite horizon (n→∞n\rarr \infinn→∞﻿)

  • Discounted Reward ✅

    Rewards are worth less the further they occur in the future. How much less is specified by the discount factor, 0≤γ≤10 \le \gamma \le 10≤γ≤1﻿
