Fundamental Frequency

Fundamental Frequency

  • the lowest frequency of a periodic waveform
    • left most in the FFT
  • what we perceive as the pitch of a note
  • the first harmonic
  • the lowest frequency sinusoidal in the sum of harmonically related frequencies, or the frequency of the difference between adjacent frequencies.

Resonances (overtone, harmonic)

Overtones or harmonics are also called resonances

Overtone

  • any higher-frequency standing wave
  • first overtone is the next higher frequency of vibration.

harmonic

  • overtone in which the frequency is an integral multiple of the frequency of the fundamental.
