How to choose hhh﻿?

Problem

UHA is impossible (too many options for hhh﻿)

Solution

Choose simple hhh﻿ s.t P(h(k)=i)=1/M\small P(h(k) = i) = 1/M P(h(k)=i)=1/M﻿

  • unrelated to any possible patterns in the data
  • depends on the key

3 Methods

  1. Modular method: k mod Mk\ mod\ Mk mod M﻿
    • choose M to avoid any patterns (ie: prime)
  1. Multiplication method: ⌊M(kA−⌊kA⌋)⌋⌊M(kA − ⌊kA⌋)⌋⌊M(kA−⌊kA⌋)⌋﻿
    • irrational 0<A<10 < A < 10<A<1﻿ to avoid patterns w’ keys
  1. New Method: Carter Wegman
    • choose hhh﻿ randomly

      h(k)=((ak+b) mod p) mod Mh(k) = ((ak + b)\ mod\ p)\ mod\ Mh(k)=((ak+b) mod p) mod M﻿

      • Requires: all keys are in {0,…,p−1}\{0,\dots,\small{p−1}\}{0,…,p−1}﻿ for a big prime ppp﻿
      • Choose M < pM < pM < p﻿ arbitrarily, (power of 2 is good)
      • Choose random a,b<p, a≠0a, b <p,\ a \ne0a,b<p, a=0﻿
