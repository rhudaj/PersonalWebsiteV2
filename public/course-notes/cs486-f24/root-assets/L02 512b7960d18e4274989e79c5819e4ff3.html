Knowledge Representation

Core Idea of Designing AI

Knowledge Base

System that houses knowledge (content) and representation (how that content is organized) in a form that the AI can access and use.

Static; holds info, but does not do anything by itself.

Knowledge

Information the AI uses to solve tasks (like facts, rules, and strategies).

Representation

How that knowledge is stored and structured within the AI system (using data structures, algorithms, etc.).

Model

How the AI interprets the knowledge base in the context of the real world.

Dynamic; uses info, interacts with world.

Level of Abstraction

How accurate is the model / how simple do you assume the world is?
