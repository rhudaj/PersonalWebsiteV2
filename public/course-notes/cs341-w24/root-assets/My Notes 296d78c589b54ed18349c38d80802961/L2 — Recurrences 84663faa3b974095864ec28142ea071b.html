Substitution (Induction)

You can use the substitution method to establish either an upper or a lower bound on a recurrence.

Steps:

  1. Guess the form of the solution using constant(s)
  1. use induction to show that the solution works.
    1. To apply the inductive hypothesis, you substitute the guessed solution for the func- tion on smaller values.
  1. Find the constant(s)

Tips & Tricks

  • for upper bound, you need enough negative stuff to cancel out the positive stuff.
  • for lower bound, you need enough positive stuff to cancel out the negative stuff.
  • If you get stuck (base case or inductive step, add lower order terms to your guess to cancel out stuff).
