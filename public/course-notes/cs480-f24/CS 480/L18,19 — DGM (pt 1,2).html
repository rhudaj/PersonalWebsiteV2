Normalizing Flows

  • Probabilistic as well, but compute exact likelihoods ⇒ more expressive latent space
  • idea: transform simple distribution ⇒ complex one with invertible transformation
    • preserves the ability to compute exact probabilities
  • With VAEs, we only optimized the lower bound, here we will explicitly compute the densities of the transformed distribution ⇒ precise representation of data

Key property: any transformation of RV preserves total probability

  • need to find how much the “area” scales as a result of the transformation
    • multivariate distributions: must be scaled by ___
    • multivariate distributions: must be scaled by the determinant of the projection matrix

General Adversarial Network (GAN)

Idea: 2 N.N’s compete with each other in a zero-sum game.

  • generator: wants to re-produce real data.
  • discriminator: wants to tell real from fake.
  • The competition drives both to improve.
    • ⇒ generator learns (approx.) the real distribution
    • ⇒ discriminator learns a binary classifier

3 Key Distributions:

Minimax Optimization

Training Objective for GAN

Train Algorithm

  • Idea: alternate between updating D & updating G, each has own objective and gradients. First, train D (gradient descent ⇒ min) for a kkk﻿ steps (for a fixed G). Then train the G (gradient ascent ⇒ max) (for a fixed D).

Problems

  • Mode collapse
    • what? GGG﻿ only re-produces only small subset (follows patterns).
    • when? GGG﻿ learns to fast compared to DDD﻿ (exploits it)
    • solutions?
      • regularization: update GGG﻿ less often, increase batch size, …
  • Vanishing Gradient
    • what? G cant learn from a perfect DDD﻿
    • when? DDD﻿ learns to fast compared to GGG﻿ ⇒ as DDD﻿ improves, gradient shrinks quickly ⇒ GGG﻿ receives less “feedback”
  • Finding Min of Max
    • what? harder to find a single optimum

how? iterative denoising (learn to reverse iteratively adding noise)

Simplified Objective

Sampling & Training

  • training: learn to predict noise that was added in forward-process
    • minimize loss (between true noise and networks prediction of the noise)
    • learn to estimate noise ⇒ learn how to sample (denoise)
  • Once trained, can sample:
    • start with pure noise (xTx_TxT​﻿)
      • e.g. every pixel drawn from gaussian
    • go backwards to x0x_0x0​﻿ — refine the noisy sample at each step.
