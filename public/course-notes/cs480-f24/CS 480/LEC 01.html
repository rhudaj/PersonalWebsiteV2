Perceptron Algorithm

Learns a (linear) Classifier (Supervised, Online, Deterministic)

  • classification ⇒ supervised
  • adjusts the model once for every observation in the dataset ⇒ online
  • Deterministic ⇒ updates weight to correct error

Properties:

  1. Perceptron converges  ⟺  \iff⟺﻿Linearly separable
    • Not linearly separable: cycles forever
  1. Solution not unique (can’t guarantee www﻿ = oracle vector at the end)
  1. Terminates when:
    1. Weights stop changing
    1. Errors stopped
